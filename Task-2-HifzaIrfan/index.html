Our Story

Buttercup Cafe started with a simple dream: create a place where every morning feels like Sunday — slow, warm, and filled with the smell of fresh coffee and baked goods.

Our name comes from the little yellow flowers that pop up unexpectedly, bringing joy to an ordinary day. That's what we aim to do with every cup and every bite.

— The Buttercup Family
